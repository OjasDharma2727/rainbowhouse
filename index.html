About Us

Rainbow Play School Shimla is a vibrant and child-friendly learning environment. Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can grow and develop to their full potential.

We believe in the holistic development of children, focusing on their physical, emotional, social, and cognitive growth. Our experienced and dedicated teachers use a play-based learning approach to make education fun and engaging.
